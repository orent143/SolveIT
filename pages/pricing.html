Pricing FAQ

What's included in the launch support?

All packages include 30–90 days of post-launch support covering bug fixes, minor adjustments, and optimization recommendations. Technical support is available via email and calls.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. If you start with a Starter plan and need more features, we can upgrade your website to the Growth or Scale plan. We'll adjust pricing based on what's already been done.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. For projects over $1,500, we offer flexible payment schedules: 30% upfront, 40% at mid-project, 30% at completion. Custom terms are available upon request.

What about hosting and maintenance?

Hosting is additional (~$30–150/month depending on needs). We can manage it for you, or you can choose your own provider. Ongoing maintenance packages are available separately.

Are there hidden fees?

No. Our pricing is transparent. We'll provide a detailed project proposal upfront so you know exactly what you're paying for. Any changes to the scope will be discussed before proceeding.

How do I know which plan is right for me?

Book a free 30-minute consultation. We'll assess your needs and recommend the best plan. Most startups start with Growth; enterprises often need Scale plans with custom features.
